taylor geospatial institute

2022-current

I am currently employed as an undergraduate researcher at the Taylor Geospatial Institute and the WATER Institute. I work closely with Dr. Jason Knouft and postdoc Dr. Ali Hassan Shabbir.

I've worked on a wide variety of tasks while working for Taylor Geospatial, some of which include data analysis of SPEI (Standardised Precipitation-Evapotranspiration Index) for the conterminous United States and Africa. Currently, I am working on utilizing SPEI and other climate data to create a tool that can be used in representing climate change on a day-to-day basis. While getting the data needed, it's also important to present this data in visuals and graphics to be presented to a wider audience.
